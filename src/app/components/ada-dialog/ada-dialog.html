Accessibility Statement for localcontactforms.com

LocalContactForms.com is committed to providing an accessible digital experience for all users, including individuals with disabilities. This Accessibility Statement explains our efforts to ensure that our website, hosted forms, and subscription-based services can be used effectively by as many people as possible, including those using assistive technologies.

Our Accessibility Approach

We strive to follow recognized accessibility standards and best practices, including elements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). Our goal is to improve usability for people with diverse abilities and to support screen readers, keyboard navigation, and alternative input methods.

Feedback and Accessibility Concerns

We welcome feedback regarding any accessibility barriers encountered on localcontactforms.com or in any client-leased form. If you experience an accessibility issue, please notify us so we can evaluate and address the concern where feasible.
